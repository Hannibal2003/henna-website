About Our Artistry

Henna Haven was founded by Priya Sharma, a third-generation mehndi artist with over 15 years of experience in creating beautiful henna designs. Our studio combines traditional techniques with contemporary creativity to deliver stunning results for every occasion.

We use only the highest quality, natural henna paste that is safe for all skin types and provides rich, long-lasting color. Each design is carefully crafted to suit your personal style and the significance of your event.
